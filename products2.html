ENGINEERED TO SURPRISE THE AUDIOPHILE IN YOU

The "final" E1000 and E1000C come embedded with driver units with a diaphragm attached to a voice coil and adhesive agent. With audio signals flowing, the diaphragm moves in micronic increments along with the voice coil that produces sound. Over time, the diaphragm moves efficiently allowing the sound to change or 'age'. The E1000 and E1000C have a small aperture, so an extended period is necessary for changes through ageing to become noticeable. It should feel as though the sound has gotten stable after 150-200 hours of use. Positive results can be gained by allowing the product to mature.
